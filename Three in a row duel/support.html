Frequently asked

How do I add a new player?

Open the Players tab and tap the + button. Type a name and tap Save. Two starter players ("Player 1" and "Player 2") are added the first time you open the app — you can rename or delete them any time.

How do I start a game against a friend?

Open the Play tab. Pick "Two Players," choose which player is X and which is O, and tap Start. Hand the phone back and forth as you tap your moves.

How do I play against the computer?

Open the Play tab and pick "vs Computer." Choose a difficulty:

  • Easy — random moves, good for younger kids.
  • Medium — blocks your winning moves and takes its own when it sees them.
  • Unbeatable — searches every possible move; the best you can do is a draw.

Why can't I win on Unbeatable?

That's the math of tic-tac-toe: if both sides play perfectly, the game always ends in a draw. The Unbeatable computer plays perfectly. Aim for the draw, or drop to Medium for a real chance to win.

How do stats and the leaderboard work?

Every completed game updates wins, losses, and draws for both players. The Leaderboard tab sorts everyone by performance so you can see who's leading the household.

How do I reset a player's stats?

Open the Players tab, tap a player, then tap Reset Stats. Their wins/losses/draws go back to zero. The player profile stays.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Everything runs on your iPhone — no internet needed at any point.

Does the app collect any data?

No. No analytics, no tracking, no ads, no accounts. See the Privacy Policy for details.
