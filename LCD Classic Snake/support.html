Frequently asked

How do I move the snake?

Swipe up, down, left, or right anywhere on the board. The snake turns immediately on the next tick. You can't reverse directly into yourself — swiping into the opposite direction is ignored.

How does scoring work?

Each food pellet you eat is worth more points on harder difficulties:

  • Easy — 10 points per food, slow speed.
  • Medium — 20 points per food, classic speed.
  • Hard — 40 points per food, fast speed.

How does the leaderboard work?

The top 10 scores for each difficulty are saved separately. Open the SCORES tab and tap a difficulty to see its leaderboard.

How do I add a new player?

Open the PLAYERS tab and type a name in the box at the top, then tap +. One starter player ("Player 1") is added the first time you open the app — you can rename or delete it any time.

What happens to old scores if I rename or delete a player?

Renaming a player doesn't change scores already on the leaderboard — each saved score keeps the player's name at the time it was set. Deleting a player removes that player's scores from the leaderboard.

How do I pause a game?

Tap the pause button at the bottom of the screen. Pausing also happens automatically if you switch apps or lock your phone.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Everything runs on your iPhone — no internet needed at any point.

Does the app collect any data?

No. No analytics, no tracking, no ads, no accounts. See the Privacy Policy for details.
