Blast off into a friendly galaxy where every problem solved flies your rocket a little farther.

Cosmic Cadets is a gentle, ad-free space adventure that helps kids ages 5–8 build real math confidence — counting, adding, subtracting and more — one short, joyful mission at a time. No ads. No in-app purchases. No accounts. No tracking. Just the fun of getting it right.

THE ADVENTURE
• Journey across a star map of 15 hand-crafted levels
• Each level is a quick mission of bite-sized problems
• Solve them to fly your rocket across the screen and earn up to 3 stars
• Friendly retries — a wrong answer never scolds; your cadet just tries again

WHAT KIDS PRACTICE
• Counting and number recognition
• Addition and subtraction
• Finding the missing number
• More or less (comparing numbers)
• First steps in multiplication (groups of 2, 5 and 10)
Younger cadets count colorful space objects; older cadets work with the numbers themselves.

GROWS WITH YOUR CHILD
• Difficulty quietly adapts to each child, one skill at a time
• Answer with ease and the numbers grow; find it tricky and it gently eases back
• So the very same game fits a brand-new counter and a confident little adder

COLLECT AND EXPLORE
• Earn stars to unlock rocket ships and cute alien companions
• A collection to come back to — with no coins to buy, ever
• Set up several kid profiles on one device, each with its own progress

FOR GROWN-UPS
• A math-gated Parent Zone shows exactly which skills your child is mastering
• Turn the sound effects and gentle background music on or off
• Absolutely no ads, no purchases, no sign-ups — and nothing ever leaves the device

PRIVACY YOU CAN TRUST
• No accounts. No sign-ups.
• No analytics. No ads. No tracking.
• Everything stays on your child's device.
• Delete the app and it all goes with it.

Whether your cadet is just learning to count or starting to multiply, Cosmic Cadets turns practice into a trip through the stars — calm, encouraging, and made to grow right alongside them.
