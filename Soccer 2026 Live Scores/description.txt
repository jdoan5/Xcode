The fast, free companion for the 2026 World Cup.

Every match, every group, every knockout round — Soccer 2026 keeps the whole tournament in your pocket. Browse the full 104-match schedule in your local time, follow your favorite teams, and check live scores and standings as they happen. Free. No accounts. No ads. No tracking.

WHAT'S INSIDE
• Full schedule of all 104 matches, grouped by day, in your local time zone
• Live scores and match status — kickoff, half-time, full time
• All 12 group tables with up-to-the-minute standings
• Knockout bracket from the Round of 32 to the Final
• Team pages with fixtures and squads
• Star teams and matches to follow the ones you care about

KICKOFF REMINDERS
• Follow any match and get a notification 15 minutes before kickoff
• Never miss your team again

WORKS OFFLINE
• The complete schedule, teams, groups, and bracket are built in
• Open the app with no signal and still see every fixture
• Live scores and standings refresh automatically when you're online

PRIVACY FIRST
• No account, no sign-up, no email
• No tracking, no analytics, no ads
• The teams and matches you follow stay on your iPhone

ABOUT THE DATA
Soccer 2026 is an independent, unofficial app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with FIFA or any football federation. Schedule, group, and team information comes from public-domain football data; live scores and standings are provided by a third-party sports data service.

Built by an independent developer who just wanted a clean, quiet way to follow the tournament.
