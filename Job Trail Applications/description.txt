The simple, private way to track every job application.

Job Trail keeps your whole job search in one place — every application, its status, the link to the posting, and the notes you need before each call. No accounts. No ads. No tracking. Everything stays on your iPhone.

WHAT'S INSIDE
• Log every application: company, role, location, date applied, and the posting URL
• Track status at a glance: Applied, Interviewing, Offer, Rejected, Withdrawn
• Color-coded status badges and one-tap filters to focus your pipeline
• Search across companies, roles, and locations
• Notes for recruiters, salary ranges, and next steps
• Tap the saved link to jump straight back to the job posting

STAY ORGANIZED
• See how long ago you applied to each role
• Filter to just your active interviews or open offers
• Sorted by most recent so your latest applications are always on top

WORKS OFFLINE
• Everything is stored on your device — no connection required, ever
• Your job search never leaves your iPhone

PRIVACY FIRST
• No account, no sign-up, no email
• No tracking, no analytics, no ads
• Delete the app and every record goes with it

Built by an independent developer who just wanted a calm, private place to run a job search.
