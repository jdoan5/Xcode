Welcome to Cosmic Cadets! This first release brings a 15-level space adventure that builds early math — counting, addition, subtraction, comparing, missing numbers, and first multiplication — with difficulty that quietly adapts to each child. Earn stars to unlock rockets and alien friends, set up several kid profiles, and check progress in the math-gated Parent Zone. Gentle sound effects and background music, both optional. No ads, no purchases, fully offline.
