Learn English the right way — one word at a time.

Learn English: Pronunciation is a focused vocabulary app for adult ESL learners who want clear native pronunciation, real example sentences, and a way to practice without ads, sign-ups, or distractions.

WHAT'S INSIDE
• 10 themed lessons covering 140+ essential words
• Alphabet A to Z with starter words
• Numbers, Colors, Animals, Food & Drink
• Family, Body, Days & Months, Time & Weather
• Common Phrases for daily conversation

EVERY WORD INCLUDES
• Native speaker pronunciation (tap to hear)
• IPA phonetic notation, shown inside the app
• A simple sound-out hint (like AP-uhl for "apple")
• A natural example sentence — also playable aloud
• A clear visual symbol

PRACTICE THAT WORKS
• Listen-and-pick quizzes — the app speaks a word, you pick it from four options
• Quiz by single lesson or mix across everything
• Score feedback after each round
• Words you answer correctly are marked as learned

SAVE & TRACK
• Star any word to save it to your Favorites
• See how many words you've learned across all lessons
• Reset progress any time

PICK YOUR ACCENT
• American English
• British English
• Australian English
• Indian English
• Adjust speech speed from slow to fast for your comfort

DESIGNED FOR LEARNERS
• Big, readable type
• High contrast colors
• VoiceOver-friendly
• Works fully offline — no Wi-Fi needed
• Portrait iPhone

PRIVACY YOU CAN TRUST
• No accounts. No sign-ups.
• No tracking. No analytics. No ads.
• No data leaves your device. Ever.
• Your favorites and progress live only on your iPhone.

Whether you're starting out or brushing up, Learn English: Pronunciation gives you the building blocks of spoken English in a calm, focused way you can return to every day.
