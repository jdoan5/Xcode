Privacy Policy

Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a calm, ad-free version of the game everyone knows. Because it's meant for kids and families, the app is designed to collect and share nothing.

Summary in one line: Tic Tac Toe collects no personal data, has no accounts, no tracking, no ads, no analytics, and makes no network requests. Player names and game stats stay on your iPhone.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not ask for or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts to create, no email to enter, and no servers to talk to.

What stays on your device

The following information is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's standard on-device database (SwiftData), and never leaves the device:

  • The names you give each player
  • Per-player stats: wins, losses, draws, win rate, games played
  • A record of each completed game (X/O players, winner, date, mode)

You can delete a player or reset their stats from the Players tab at any time. Deleting the app removes everything.

Third-party services

None. Tic Tac Toe does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

Network access

The app does not make network requests. The game, the leaderboard, and the computer opponents all run entirely on your iPhone.

Children

Tic Tac Toe is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). It does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app starts using a feature that affects privacy — we'll update this page and clearly note what changed.
