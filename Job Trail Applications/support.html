Frequently asked

How do I add a job?

Tap the + button in the top-right of the list, fill in the company (the only required field) plus any of role, location, date applied, status, link, and notes, then tap Save.

How do I change an application's status?

Open the job and use the Status picker — set it to Applied, Interviewing, Offer, Rejected, or Withdrawn. The color-coded badge on the list updates right away.

How do I edit or delete a job?

Tap a job and choose Edit to change any field. To delete, swipe the row left in the list.

How do I filter or search?

Use the status chips at the top of the list to show just one status (for example, only your active interviews), and the search bar to find a job by company, role, or location.

How do I open the original job posting?

If you saved a link with the job, open the job and tap the link to jump to the posting in your browser. You don't need to include "https://" — the app adds it for you.

Does the app sync across my devices or to iCloud?

This version stores everything on the iPhone you're using, with no cloud copy. That keeps your search completely private. If you'd like iCloud sync in a future update, let me know.

Does the app work offline?

Yes — completely. Job Trail makes no network connections and works with no signal.

Does the app collect any data?

No. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no ads. The only data is what you type about your own applications, kept on your iPhone. See the Privacy Policy.
