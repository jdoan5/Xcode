## Why I Build Apps

I build apps because I love turning small frustrations into thoughtful, well-crafted tools. Each project here started as a problem I wanted solved, and grew into something I felt was worth sharing on the App Store. My goal is always the same: make something genuinely useful, keep it clean and intuitive, and respect the people who use it — including their privacy and their time.

This repository is a home for that work. **Each folder contains one app** that's live on the App Store, along with its details, screenshots, privacy notes, and how to get support.

## Feedback Welcome

I genuinely value feedback — it's how these apps get better. If you have an idea, found a bug, or just want to share how you're using one of the apps:

- **Open an issue** in this repository
- **Leave a review** on the App Store
- **Reach out** using the contact details below

No suggestion is too small. Thank you for taking the time. 🙏

## Privacy

I take privacy seriously. As a rule, my apps:

- Collect only the minimum data needed to function
- Avoid third-party tracking and advertising SDKs wherever possible
- Keep your data on your device unless a feature explicitly requires otherwise

Each app folder contains its own **Privacy Policy** with specifics for that app. If anything is unclear, please reach out.
