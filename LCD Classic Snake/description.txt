The classic snake game, with a retro LCD soul.

LCD Classic Snake is a calm, ad-free take on the arcade game everyone grew up with — built for quick rounds in line, on the couch, or anywhere your phone goes. No ads. No accounts. No tracking. Just the game.

HOW TO PLAY
• Swipe up, down, left, or right to steer
• Eat the food pellet to grow and score
• Don't hit the walls. Don't bite yourself.

THREE DIFFICULTIES
• Easy — slow tick, 10 points per food. A relaxed warm-up.
• Medium — classic arcade speed, 20 points per food.
• Hard — fast and unforgiving, 40 points per food. For chasing the top spot.

NAMED-PLAYER LEADERBOARD
• Add a real name for each person who plays
• Top 10 scores tracked per difficulty
• Compete with the household, the office, or just yourself
• See your personal best on the Play tab before every game

EVERY SCORE REMEMBERED
• Each saved score keeps the player's name at the time it was set, so renames and deletions never break the board
• Per-player totals: games played and cumulative score
• Reset by deleting a player from the Players tab

DESIGNED LIKE A HANDHELD
• Green-on-black LCD aesthetic
• Monospaced display font and pixel-sharp grid
• Big swipe target, clear HUD
• Pause / resume button
• Auto-pauses when you switch apps or lock your phone
• Portrait iPhone

PRIVACY YOU CAN TRUST
• No accounts. No sign-ups.
• No analytics. No ads. No tracking.
• Every player and score stays on your iPhone.
• Delete the app and it all goes with it.

Whether you're killing five minutes in line or trying to dethrone the kid with the suspiciously high Hard score, LCD Classic Snake gives you the original game in a sharp little retro package.
