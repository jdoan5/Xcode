Privacy Policy

Learn English: Pronunciation

Learn English: Pronunciation is built for adult learners of English. Privacy and a calm learning experience matter to us, so the app is designed to collect and share nothing.

Summary in one line: Learn English: Pronunciation collects no personal data, has no accounts, no tracking, no ads, no analytics, and makes no network requests.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not ask for or transmit any personal information.

What stays on your device

The following preferences are stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's standard UserDefaults system, and never leave the device:

  • Words you star as favorites
  • Words you've heard at least once (the "learned" counter)
  • Grammar topics you've opened and fill-in-the-blank drills you've completed
  • Your chosen voice accent (US, UK, AU, or IN)
  • Your chosen speech speed

You can clear your progress at any time from the Settings tab. Deleting the app removes everything.

Third-party services

None. Learn English: Pronunciation does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

Network access

The app does not make network requests. All lessons, audio playback, and content work fully offline.

Children

Learn English: Pronunciation is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). It does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app starts using a feature that affects privacy — we'll update this page and clearly note what changed.
