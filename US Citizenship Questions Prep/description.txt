Pass the 2025 U.S. citizenship civics test with confidence.

Civics Test 2025 puts all 128 official USCIS questions and answers (the 2025 version) in your pocket — with a practice test that works like the real interview, a fill-in-the-blank mode that makes you produce the answer yourself, and clear notes on the answers that change with elections. No accounts. No ads. No tracking. Everything stays on your iPhone.

THE REAL TEST, SIMULATED
• 20 random questions, multiple choice — 12 correct passes, just like the real oral exam
• Ends early once the result is decided, exactly like the interview
• Review every miss with the correct answer and the full acceptable list

FILL IN THE BLANK
• Type the answer the way you would say it to the officer
• Forgiving grading: "27" matches "twenty-seven (27)", "Jefferson" matches "(Thomas) Jefferson"
• Questions that ask for two, three, or five answers give you a field for each
• Mark an answer correct yourself when your wording differed — it's an oral test

STUDY ALL 128
• Browse by official topic: American Government, American History, Symbols and Holidays
• Search by question number, question text, or any answer
• Filter the 20 starred 65/20 questions at any time

ANSWERS THAT CHANGE WITH ELECTIONS
• President, Vice President, Speaker of the House, Chief Justice, your senators, your representative, your governor, and your state capital — the app flags every one of them
• Save the current names in My Officials and practice those questions like any other
• Built-in link to the official USCIS test-updates page so you can verify before your interview

65/20 SPECIAL CONSIDERATION
• One toggle switches the whole app to the official 20-question study list for applicants 65 or older with 20+ years as permanent residents (10 asked, 6 to pass)

WORKS OFFLINE, PRIVACY FIRST
• The whole question bank is built in — no connection required, ever
• No account, no sign-up, no tracking, no ads
• The only things stored are your settings and the official names you type, locally on your iPhone
