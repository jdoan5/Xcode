Frequently asked

How does my child play?

On the Adventure map, tap the glowing (unlocked) planet to start a mission, then tap the big number button with the right answer. Each correct answer flies the rocket farther; finishing a level earns up to 3 stars and unlocks the next planet. A wrong answer is gentle — it just invites another try.

Does the game get harder or easier on its own?

Yes. Cosmic Cadets quietly adapts to each child, one skill at a time. When answers come easily the numbers grow; when a skill is tricky it eases back — so the same game suits a brand-new counter and a confident adder.

What does my child practice?

Counting, addition, subtraction, finding a missing number, comparing numbers (more or less), and first steps in multiplication (groups of 2, 5, and 10). Younger children count colorful objects on screen; older children work with the numbers.

How do I add another child?

Open the Cadets tab and tap "Add Cadet." Each cadet gets their own name, avatar, color, and separate progress. Tap a cadet to make them the active player.

What is the Parent Zone, and how do I open it?

Tap the gear icon at the top-right of the Adventure map. It's behind a simple math question (so a grown-up opens it). Inside you'll find a per-skill progress dashboard, switches for sound and music, and an option to reset a cadet's progress.

How do I turn the music or sound effects off?

Open the Parent Zone. "Mute sounds & haptics" turns off the effects, and "Background music" has its own on/off switch and volume slider. Music also follows your phone's silent switch.

Is there anything to buy?

No. Every rocket and companion is earned with stars from playing. There are no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no ads — ever.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Everything runs on the device — no internet is needed at any point.

Does the app collect any data?

No. No analytics, no tracking, no ads, no accounts. Everything stays on the device. See the Privacy Policy for details.
