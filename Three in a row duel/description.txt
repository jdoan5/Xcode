The classic 3-in-a-row, done right.

Tic Tac Toe is a calm, clean take on the game everyone already knows — built for quick matches at the table, in the back seat, or anywhere your phone goes. No ads. No accounts. No tracking. Just the game.

TWO WAYS TO PLAY
• Two players on one device — pass and play with anyone nearby
• vs Computer — pick a difficulty and see how you stack up

THREE COMPUTER DIFFICULTIES
• Easy — plays at random, perfect for younger kids
• Medium — blocks your winning moves and takes its own when it sees them
• Unbeatable — full minimax search, the way the math says: you can draw, but you cannot win

NAMED-PLAYER LEADERBOARD

• See who's actually the household champion
