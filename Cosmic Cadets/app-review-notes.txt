1. DEVICES AND OS TESTED
Simulator: iPhone 17, iPhone 17 Pro, iPhone 17 Pro Max (all iOS 26.5).
Minimum deployment: iOS 17.0. iPhone only, portrait.

2. PURPOSE, AUDIENCE, PROBLEM, VALUE
Cosmic Cadets is a single-player educational math game for children ages 5–8. The child journeys across a star map of 15 levels; each level is a short mission of math problems (counting, addition, subtraction, missing number, comparing, and intro multiplication in groups of 2/5/10). Correct answers fly a rocket across a progress track and earn 1–3 stars; stars unlock cosmetic rockets and alien companions. Difficulty adapts per child, per skill. Several child profiles ("cadets") can be kept on one device. Audience is young children and their parents. Free, no ads, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases. Rated 4+.

3. SETUP, ACCESS, CREDENTIALS
No login, no account, no email, no demo credentials — the app has no server backend. On first launch a default cadet "Explorer" is created so play can begin immediately. The reviewer can rename or delete it and add others from the CADETS tab.

4. HOW TO REVIEW
Open the app → the ADVENTURE map is shown → tap the highlighted (unlocked) planet → answer the problems by tapping the big number buttons → finishing shows a star score → return to the map and the next level unlocks. The COLLECTION tab shows rockets and companions (locked ones show the star cost). The CADETS tab manages child profiles. The PARENT ZONE (gear icon, top-right of the map) is behind a simple parental gate: a multiplication problem is shown on screen — tap its correct answer to enter. Inside are a per-skill progress dashboard, a sound/haptics mute, a background-music toggle and volume, and a progress reset. Everything works with no network connection.

5. EXTERNAL SERVICES / TOOLS / PLATFORMS
None. The app makes no network requests at any point and uses only first-party Apple frameworks:
- SwiftUI — user interface
- SwiftData — on-device persistence for cadets, level progress, and skill stats
- AVFoundation — playback of bundled sound effects and music
- StoreKit — only to optionally request an App Store review at a positive moment

No third-party SDK of any kind. No analytics, no advertising, no auth provider, no payment processor, no cloud sync, no CloudKit, no Game Center.

6. KIDS / PRIVACY
The app is appropriate for the Kids category. It collects no data and transmits nothing off the device — there is no analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party content. The only persistence is on-device (SwiftData for progress; UserDefaults for settings such as the active cadet, sound/music preferences, and the review-prompt record — declared in PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy under reason CA92.1). The Parent Zone, the only "settings/management" surface, sits behind a math-based parental gate. There are no external links, no purchases, and no user-generated content or communication features. App Privacy answer: Data Not Collected.

7. THIRD-PARTY MATERIAL
None. All visuals, code, and copy are original to this app. The app icon and in-app art are drawn in code/Core Graphics. All sound effects and the background music are synthesized procedurally (no third-party audio, samples, fonts, or icons are included). The "math game" and "space" themes are generic with no trademarked elements. No authorization or third-party credential is required to publish this app.
