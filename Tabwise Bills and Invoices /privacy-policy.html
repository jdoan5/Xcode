Privacy Policy

Invoice Tracker

Invoice Tracker helps you keep track of your own bills, invoices, and payments. It is built to collect nothing about you — no account, no tracking, no ads, no analytics, and no network connection at all.

Summary in one line: Everything you enter — your records, amounts, notes, and any attachments — is stored only on your device. The app has no accounts, contacts no servers, and sends nothing anywhere.

What we collect

Nothing about you personally. There are no accounts to create, no email to enter, and we operate no server that could receive your data. The app uses no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

What stays on your device

Everything you put into Invoice Tracker is stored locally on your device and never leaves it:

  • Your records — amounts, payee/client, category, payment method, check numbers, payment URLs, reference numbers, dates, status, and notes (stored with Apple's on-device SwiftData)
  • Any attachments you add, such as photos of receipts or PDF invoices (stored on the device with the record)
  • Your preferences, such as your chosen currency and whether app lock is on (stored with Apple's UserDefaults)

Deleting the app removes all of it. There is no cloud copy and no backup held by us.

Network use

Invoice Tracker makes no network requests and works entirely offline. The app sends nothing on its own. If you save a payment URL with a record and tap it, your device opens that link in your browser — that is a normal web request made by your browser, not by this app, and only happens when you choose to tap it.

Notifications

Due-date reminders are optional. If you turn them on, the app asks your permission and then schedules local notifications on your device for unpaid records that have a due date. These reminders are created and delivered entirely on your device; no due dates or record details are sent anywhere.

Photos and files

When you attach a receipt or invoice, you pick it through the system Photos picker or the system document picker. The app only receives the specific items you choose; it is not granted access to your full photo library or file system. Imported attachments are stored locally with the record.

Face ID / Touch ID

You can optionally turn on an app lock. It uses Apple's LocalAuthentication only to confirm it's you before showing your records. The app does not access, receive, or store any biometric data — that stays within the system.

Not financial advice

Invoice Tracker provides general record-keeping only. It is not professional financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. It is a tool for organizing your own records — it does not connect to any bank or account and never moves, sends, or requests money.

Children

Invoice Tracker is a general-audience app and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13. (It collects nothing from anyone.)

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date below. Because the app collects nothing and uses no network, changes are expected to be rare.
