Frequently asked

How do I hear a word?

Open any lesson, tap a word card, then tap the "Word" button. Tap "Sentence" to hear the example sentence aloud.

How do I save a word?

While viewing a lesson detail, tap the star icon next to a selected word. Saved words live in the Favorites tab.

How do I change the voice accent?

Open the Settings tab. Pick American, British, Australian, or Indian English under Voice.

How do I slow down the speech?

In Settings, drag the Speech Speed slider toward the left. Tap Preview to hear how it sounds.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. All lessons, audio, and quizzes work fully offline. The app never makes network requests.

Does the app collect any data?

No. No analytics, no tracking, no ads, no accounts. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I reset my progress?

Settings tab → "Reset learned words". Your favorites stay; only the learned counter resets.

Will more lessons be added?

Yes. Future updates will add more themed lessons and additional practice modes.
