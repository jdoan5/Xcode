Frequently asked

How do I add a bill or invoice?

On the Invoices tab, tap the + button and choose Money Out (a bill you pay) or Money In (an invoice or payment you receive). Enter the amount, who it's with, a category, and how it was paid, then save.

How do I record a check or an online payment?

Set the payment method to Check and a field appears for the check number. Choose Online (or any method) and you can paste the payment or receipt URL, which becomes a tappable link on the record. You can also add a reference or invoice number.

What do the statuses mean?

Each record is Pending, Partially Paid, or Paid. If something is unpaid past its due date it's shown as Overdue. Use "Mark as paid" on a record to settle it.

Can bills repeat automatically?

Yes. Set a record to repeat weekly, every two weeks, monthly, quarterly, or yearly. When you mark it paid, the next occurrence is created for you with the dates moved forward.

How do reminders work? Are they private?

Turn on "Due date reminders" in Settings and the app will ask for notification permission, then schedule local reminders before your unpaid records are due. The reminders are created and delivered entirely on your device — nothing is sent anywhere.

Can I attach a receipt or PDF?

Yes. When adding or editing a record, attach a photo (a receipt or a picture of the check) or a file such as a PDF invoice. Attachments are stored on your device with the record.

How do I see totals and tax-deductible amounts?

The Dashboard shows what you owe, what's owed to you, your net position, and charts of the last six months and spending by category. Flag a record as tax-deductible and the Dashboard shows your year-to-date deductible total.

Can I export my records?

Yes — Settings has a CSV export you can share with your accountant or open in a spreadsheet.

Can I lock the app?

Yes. Turn on Face ID / Touch ID in Settings to require authentication each time the app opens.

Does the app work offline? Does it collect any data?

It works completely offline and makes no network connections. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no ads. The only data is what you enter, kept on your device. See the Privacy Policy.
