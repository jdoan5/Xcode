Build better habits, right from your wrist.

Habit Tracker is a calm, focused habit tracker made only for Apple Watch — no phone required. Glance at today's habits, tap to check one off, and watch your streak grow. No accounts, no ads, no tracking. Everything stays on your wrist.

MADE FOR APPLE WATCH
• A true watch-only app — open it and start, no iPhone companion needed
• Big, tappable rings sized for a quick check-in
• A tap completes a habit; the ring fills and you feel a satisfying haptic
• Built for the few seconds you actually have

TRACK WHAT MATTERS
• Add any habit with an emoji, a color, and a schedule
• Choose every day, weekdays, or pick exact days of the week
• Set a daily target — once a day, or several (water, stretch breaks, vitamins)
• Tap through your target; one more tap resets the day if you slip

SEE YOUR STREAKS
• Current streak and best streak for every habit
• A 30-day completion rate so you know how you're really doing
• This-week strip and a 28-day history grid at a glance
• Today screen shows how many habits are done so far

QUICK AND QUIET
• Swipe a habit for its full detail and history
• Manage, reorder, and archive habits from one screen
• Clean dark design that looks right on the watch face
• Haptics that confirm without nagging

PRIVACY YOU CAN TRUST
• No accounts. No sign-ups. No cloud.
• No analytics. No ads. No tracking.
• Every habit and streak stays on your Apple Watch.

Whether you're drinking more water, reading every night, or finally keeping that streak alive, Habit Tracker keeps the whole thing on your wrist — simple, private, and always one tap away.
