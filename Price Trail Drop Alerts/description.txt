Stop refreshing product pages. Price Trail watches them for you.

Paste any product link — Amazon, Walmart, Best Buy, Target, and most other stores — and Price Trail checks the price for you, quietly, in the background. The moment it drops, you get a notification. That's the whole idea, and it's free.

TRACK ANY PRODUCT
• Paste a link, and the app finds the product's name, photo, and current price automatically
• Prices are re-checked while you're in the app and periodically in the background
• Everything works without an account — nothing to sign up for

PRICE-DROP ALERTS
• Get notified on any price drop, or set a target price and hear only when it's crossed
• Alerts tell you the old price, the new price, and which store

COMPARE STORES
• Track the same product at multiple stores and see them side by side
• The cheapest store is highlighted, always
• Open any store's page with one tap when you're ready to buy

PRICE HISTORY
• A clear chart of every store's price over time — 1 month, 3 months, or everything
• See your target price right on the chart
• Know instantly whether today's price is actually a deal or just marketing

OUTLOOK: SHOULD YOU WAIT?
• An on-device estimate of where the price is heading, computed from the product's own history
• "Price drop likely", "stable", or "may rise" — with a confidence meter and a plain-English explanation
• Calculated entirely on your device. No cloud AI reading your shopping list.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking
• The app talks only to the store pages you add
• Your product list syncs between your iPhone and iPad through your own private iCloud
• Works on iPhone and iPad

Price Trail is for anyone who's ever thought "I'll buy it when it goes on sale" — and then missed the sale.
