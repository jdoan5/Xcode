Privacy Policy

LCD Classic Snake

LCD Classic Snake is a single-player retro snake game. Because it's meant for casual play with no logins or social features, the app is designed to collect and share nothing.

Summary in one line: LCD Classic Snake collects no personal data, has no accounts, no tracking, no ads, no analytics, and makes no network requests. Player names and scores stay on your iPhone.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not ask for or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts to create, no email to enter, and no servers to talk to.

What stays on your device

The following information is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's standard on-device database (SwiftData), and never leaves the device:

  • The names you give each player
  • Per-player totals: games played and cumulative score
  • Each saved score (player name snapshot, difficulty, score, food eaten, date)

You can delete a player or any individual score from inside the app at any time. Deleting the app removes everything.

Third-party services

None. LCD Classic Snake does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

Network access

The app does not make network requests. The game and the leaderboard run entirely on your iPhone.

Children

LCD Classic Snake is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). It does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app starts using a feature that affects privacy — we'll update this page and clearly note what changed.
