The calm caregiver app for medications and appointments.

Family Medical helps you look after the people you care about — parents, partners, kids, or yourself — without juggling sticky notes, calendar pop-ups, or spreadsheets. Track every medication, refill date, injection, doctor visit, and insurance call in one quiet place. Free. No accounts. No tracking. Nothing leaves your phone.

WHAT'S INSIDE
• Add every family member with a name, color, and avatar
• Track medications: pills, injections, liquids, inhalers, drops, topicals
• Save dosage, schedule, pharmacy, Rx number, prescriber, and notes
• Watch refill dates with clear urgency — overdue, refill now, this week, planned
• Log appointments: doctor visits, lab work, insurance calls, pharmacy pickups, injection days
• Mark appointments complete with a tap

TODAY VIEW
• See everything coming up across the whole family in one scroll
• Sorted into Overdue, Today, This Week, and Later
• Color-coded by urgency so the eye finds the important stuff first

WRITE WITH APPLE INTELLIGENCE
• Draft polite, short messages to doctors, pharmacies, and insurance
• Refill requests, reschedule notes, insurance questions, general updates
• Generated entirely on device — your medical details never leave your iPhone
• Falls back to clean templates if Apple Intelligence isn't enabled
• Copy or Share the draft straight to Mail, Messages, or any app

BUILT FOR CAREGIVERS
• Big, readable type
• Clear visual hierarchy, friendly color coding
• Works fully offline — no Wi-Fi needed
• Portrait iPhone

PRIVACY YOU CAN TRUST
• No accounts. No sign-ups. No cloud.
• No tracking. No analytics. No ads.
• AI runs on your device using Apple Intelligence.
• Every name, medication, and appointment stays on your iPhone.
• Delete the app and everything is gone.

IMPORTANT
Family Medical is an organizer for the information you already keep — it does not provide medical advice, diagnose, or replace any conversation with a clinician or pharmacist. Always follow the directions on your prescriptions and from your healthcare providers.

Family Medical is an independent app built by a caregiver, for caregivers.
